I am a Ph.D. candidate majoring in Computer Science and Research Associate in State University of New York (SUNY), Binghamton, the USA since the fall semester 2019. I obtained my undergraduate and master degree from Chongqing University, China in 2016 and 2019, separately.

I am passionate about solving issues and creating unexplored values for people. I was the only one who won the HUAWEI First Scholarship at Chongqing University (master, 2018), the only Academic Star in 2018 at College of Computer Science at Chongqing University, as well as the only Academic Star in 2019 at BDSC group. I have eight papers as the first author, including PerCom and IEEE TITS. I am open to all kinds of academic communication.
